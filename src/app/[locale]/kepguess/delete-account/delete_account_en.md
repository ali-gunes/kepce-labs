# Account Deletion Request

At KepGuess, we value our users' privacy and data control. If you wish to delete your account and associated data, you can submit your request by following the steps below.

## How Can I Delete My Account?

To request the deletion of your account, please follow these steps:

1.  Send an email to **kepguess@kepcelabs.com** from the email address associated with your KepGuess account.
2.  Use **"Account Deletion Request"** as the subject line.
3.  Include your **Username** in the body of the email.

Once your request is received, it will be processed within 7-14 business days, and your account will be permanently deleted. A confirmation email will be sent to you once the deletion process is complete.

## What Data Will Be Deleted?

When your account is deleted, the following data will be permanently removed:

*   **Profile Information:** Your name, email address, and profile picture.
*   **Account Balance:** All KEP (virtual currency) balances and transaction history.
*   **Game Data:** Your prediction history, stakes, daily streaks, and completed missions.
*   **Device Information:** Device identifiers associated with your account.

## Data Retention and Additional Periods

Once the account deletion process is complete, your personal data is fully removed from our active databases. However, due to legal obligations, fraud prevention, or technical requirements:

*   Your data may remain stored in our secure backup systems in an encrypted format for a maximum of **30 days**. After this period, it will be automatically purged from backups.
*   Anonymized usage statistics (general data that does not contain any personal information) may be retained to improve our service quality.

For any questions, feel free to contact us at **kepguess@kepcelabs.com**.
