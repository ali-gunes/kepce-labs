# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: December 17, 2024**

## Introduction

Welcome to KepGuess ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use the KepGuess mobile application (the "App"). By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Information You Provide

- **Account Information**: When you sign up for KepGuess using Google Authentication, we collect your Google account email address and basic profile information.
- **User-Generated Content**: Information you provide when participating in predictions, including your prediction choices and bet amounts.

### 2. Automatically Collected Information

- **Usage Data**: Information about how you use the App, including predictions viewed, bets placed, daily streaks, missions completed, and in-app purchases.
- **Device Information**: We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Log Data**: When you access the App, our servers automatically record information including your IP address, app version, access times, and pages viewed.

### 3. In-App Currency (KEP)

We track your KEP balance, transactions, earnings, and spending within the App. KEP is a virtual currency for entertainment purposes only and has no real-world monetary value.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

- **Providing Services**: To operate and maintain the App, process your predictions and bets, manage your KEP balance, and deliver daily prediction packages.
- **Personalization**: To personalize your experience and recommend predictions based on your interests and categories.
- **Communication**: To send you push notifications about closing predictions, prediction results, daily rewards, missions, and important updates about the App.
- **Email Notifications**: To send you email notifications about predictions closing soon (30 minutes before closure) if you have opted in.
- **Analytics and Improvement**: To analyze usage patterns, track app performance, and improve our services.
- **Security**: To detect, prevent, and address fraud, abuse, security issues, and technical problems.
- **Legal Compliance**: To comply with applicable laws, regulations, and legal processes.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information. We may share your information in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers**: We use Supabase for backend services, which may process your data on our behalf under strict confidentiality obligations.
- **Advertising Partners**: We use Google AdMob for rewarded advertisements. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage information in accordance with Google's privacy policies.
- **Legal Requirements**: We may disclose your information if required by law, court order, or governmental request, or to protect our rights, property, or safety.
- **Business Transfers**: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred to the acquiring entity.

## Data Retention

We retain your personal information for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You may request account deletion at any time by contacting us at kepguess@kepcelabs.com. Upon deletion, we will remove your personal information from our active databases, though some data may be retained in backup systems for a limited period.

## Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

## Children's Privacy

KepGuess is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us at kepguess@kepcelabs.com.

## Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

- **Access**: Request access to the personal information we hold about you.
- **Correction**: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete personal information.
- **Deletion**: Request deletion of your personal information.
- **Objection**: Object to the processing of your personal information.
- **Data Portability**: Request a copy of your personal information in a structured, machine-readable format.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at kepguess@kepcelabs.com.
